Our Philosophy

Cooking is an act of love

Since 1994, Maison Rouge has been a quiet corner of Chennai where French culinary tradition meets the bold spirit of South India. Our kitchen is led by Chef Arjun Ramasamy, who trained under the great kitchens of Lyon before returning home to create something entirely his own.

We source from local farmers, spice merchants, and fishermen who share our belief — that the finest ingredients need very little done to them.
